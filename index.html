ABOUT Nexus Summit

The CSE Nexus Summit brings students together with experienced professionals in computer science, technology, and entrepreneurship. Its goal is to offer real insight into the industry through inspiring talks, career stories, and practical advice shared by leaders and innovators. The day ends with a Networking Party, where students, alumni, and guests can connect freely, exchange ideas, and showcase projects in a friendly and dynamic atmosphere.
